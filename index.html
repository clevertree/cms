About Us

It's all about the homeless. From local to nationwide and elsewhere, our focus is on homelessness issues and coming up with solutions with the local community. Arizona Friends of Homeless is a non-profit 501c3 organization which serves the homeless through daily outreach, connections to local services, shelters, and programs, and provides awareness to the general public, members, advocates, donors, and volunteers on homelessness issues for Arizonans. Arizona Friends of Homeless hands out food, clothing, blankets, towels, hygiene packs, ponchos, socks, back packs, bikes, shoes, pet food and more relief items to the homeless at various locations within Phoenix where need is great.
